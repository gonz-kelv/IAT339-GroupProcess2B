FALL 2015 LAUNCH

NOVEMBER 2015:HYPEBEAST

SoCal footwear label introduces a new silhouette to its sneaker lineup ahead of the fall season. The all-new Frazier shoe utilizes a mix of traditional athletic elements and contemporary aesthetics, resulting in a stripped-back, minimalist design. The high-top’s upper is constructed premium leather and features tonal waxed nylon lacing and neoprene accents with subtle branding placed at the tongue. Sitting atop of a molded EVA footbed for increased breathability and a rubber outsole, purchase a pair in a trio of stylish colorways for $150 USD from the brand’s webstore now.

DECEMBER 2015:THE KLIDE FRAZIER

The KLIDE Frazier is a subtle and minimalist looking high top that still manages to get the job done, and then some. Set to be a part of KLIDE’s Autumn/Winter, let’s take a closer look at what this high top has to offer. The Frazier is composed out of a full grain leather upper that is the accented by the hits of suede and neoprene. The upper will then be placed on an EVA footbed to make sure that you’re not just stylish, but comfortable as well. Also placed underneath is KLIDE’s custom SP (Standard Premium) protruding tread sole. Tonal laces along with a white midsole complete the look of the high top Frazier. With your choice of either ruby, white or black, let us know which pair you’re looking forward to getting.

SPRING 2016 LAUNCH

JANUARY 2016:TOWN - HAMILTON SP

Every once in a while we have to come home from the outdoors and class it up around town. That doesn’t mean the experience should be painful, though. Just take the KLIDE Hamilton SP, for example. A super-clean leather upper with suede accents has you looking sharp, but a molded EVA footbed for breathability and a protruding tread for improved grip makes the Hamilton SP both comfortable and functional, keeping feet happy without sacrificing that urban aesthetic.

SUMMER 2016 LAUNCH

MAY 2016:FIRE CREAM

"KLIDE drops its range of footwear for the 2015 summer season with five new colorways for three classic styles. The Strayhorn Textile “Midnight Batik” is a mid-top sneaker that’s dressed in a striking mixed blue pattern and features a soft textured canvas upper with suede accents, a rubber cupsole and cotton cord laces. The Ellington Textile has a printed canvas upper, full grain leather accents, a rubber cupsole and waxed cotton cord laces.

JULY 2016:DEEP NAVY

This silhouette is offered in two colorways: “Fire Cream,” which has a red floral pattern on a white base, and “Midnight Cream,” which is blue floral on white. And finally, there’s the Bruce, a lightweight and breathable model that’s constructed with open cell mesh for its perforated upper, canvas binding, a moisture wicking mesh lined footbed, an EVA cupsole and flat nylon laces. This comes in two colorways: “Black Deep Navy,” which has black mesh over a blue base, and “Deep Navy Lime,” which is dark blue over bright green."
